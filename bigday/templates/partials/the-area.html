Things to Do

If you have never been to the National Capital Region (NCR/Washington DC) there's tons of things to do.

Tour the Pentagon

The tours might reopen before the wedding, so if they do, you need to check it out. The Pentagon is a fascinating place that is full of history.

Museums

The DC area is home to some of the world's best museums. Sure there are classics like Air & Space or the Natural history Museum. But lesser known gems include the National Building Museum, and the the Folger Shakespeare Library.

Food

DC is a foodie town. You cannot go wrong with the site of Danny and Lisette's first date Tryst or the site of their second date The Hamilton. If you like sushi, DC is home to two Michelin-starred sushi restaurants: Sushi Taro, and Sushi Nakazawa.
